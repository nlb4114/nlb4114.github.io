About Me

Hi, I'm Nick Backes, also referred to as Backpacker Backes. I am a 29 year old graduate student that spends his free time traveling the world every chance he gets. So far I have traveled to 36 countries across 4 continents.

A Brief History

Raised in a small farm town in Southern Illinois, I felt that I needed so much more than what my small town had to offer. I needed ot get away

After graduating from the University of Illinois, I joined the Navy as an Officer in the Civil Engineer Corps where I was actived duty for 5.5 and deployed twice. This introduced me to the life of traveling and experiencing different cultures
